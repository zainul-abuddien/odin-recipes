Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with chicken, spices, and basmati rice. It is popular in India and is often served during special occasions.

This dish has a rich aroma because of spices like cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and biryani masala.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken pieces
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Curd
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice.
  2. Marinate chicken with curd, spices, and salt.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown.
  4. Cook the chicken with tomatoes and spices.
  5. Boil the rice until it is almost cooked.
  6. Layer rice and chicken in a pot.
  7. Cover and cook on low flame for some time.
  8. Serve hot with raita.
