Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft and fluffy breakfast food made with flour, milk, eggs, and sugar. They are usually served with honey, syrup, or fruits.

This recipe is simple and beginner-friendly. It can be made quickly with basic kitchen ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and egg to the bowl.
  3. Mix everything until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan and add a little butter.
  5. Pour the batter into the pan.
  6. Cook until bubbles appear on top.
  7. Flip the pancake and cook the other side.
  8. Serve with honey or fruits.
