Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish made with layers of pasta, sauce, cheese, and vegetables or meat. It is baked until the cheese becomes soft and golden.

This recipe is simple and perfect for beginners because it uses basic ingredients and easy steps.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables or meat
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna sheets until they become soft.
  2. Prepare the tomato sauce with vegetables or meat.
  3. Place one layer of lasagna sheet in a baking dish.
  4. Add sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Repeat the layers.
  6. Bake until the cheese melts and turns golden.
  7. Serve hot.
